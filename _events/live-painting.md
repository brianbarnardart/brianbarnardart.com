Live painting is at the heart of Brian's practice. Setting up a canvas in the middle of a performance — music pulsing, crowd moving — and capturing the energy before it disappears is the whole game.

The finished piece reflects the moment, not just a scene. You can see the choices made in real time, the brushwork that matches the tempo of the music, the colors chosen under stage lights.
