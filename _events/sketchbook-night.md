Sketchbook Night is a recurring gathering for artists of any level to sit together, open their sketchbooks, and draw. No agenda, no critique — just the practice of making marks in good company.

Brian has been hosting and attending sketch nights for years. There's something specific about the low-pressure, side-by-side energy of a sketch night that loosens things up and leads to some of the most honest work.
