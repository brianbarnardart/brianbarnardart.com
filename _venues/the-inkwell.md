**The Inkwell** ([(website)](https://www.theinkwellamelia.com)) is a bar, music venue, gallery, and art class space...all rolled into one.  It has a relaxed, creative vibe that fits right in with Brian's live-painting work. Whether it's an open-mic night or a full band, the atmosphere here always inspires.

{% include figure.html image="/assets/venues/the-inkwell-2.jpg" caption="An evening at The Inkwell" side="left" %}

Located a short 7-minute drive from downtown Fernandina, the Inkwell offers plentiful free parking, and an assortment of wine and beer selections.  There's a small food menu, or the next-door neighbor [Savannah Pizza Company](https://order.toasttab.com/online/savannah-pizza-company-fernandina-1925-s-14th-st) can bring your orders right to you.

[Check The Inkwell's calendar](https://www.theinkwellamelia.com/events) to see what's coming up!
