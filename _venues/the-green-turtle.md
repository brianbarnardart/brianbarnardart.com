It's a happy coincidence that Amelia Island's **The Green Turtle** ([(facebook)](https://www.facebook.com/greenturtletavern1/)) is terrapin-themed, as Brian's longstanding interest in painting turtles (tracing back to the Grateful Dead's [Terrapin Station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terrapin_Station)) fit perfectly.

Known around town simply as *"The Turtle"*, it's a relaxed bar and venue that brings together locals and tourists for outdoor music 7 nights a week...either on the porch or on the stage.  Brian was comissioned to paint the murals on the fences, which sets the mood for magical nights.

If it's a Friday or Saturday night, you're likely to find Brian at his easel, up by the band, chatting and painting.  (Or if a friend is playing on a school night you might find him there, too.)  Come say hi!
