Interested in a piece made specifically for you?

### What Brian Does Well

- **Live scenes** — musicians, performers, crowds, the energy of a moment
- **Portraits and figures** — people, animals, characters real and imagined
- **Narrative and fantastical imagery** — work that tells a story or inhabits a specific world
- **Site-specific work** — paintings made for a particular place or event

### Things To Consider

- What kind of space is this for?
- Are there colors it needs to work with, or colors you want to avoid?
- Is there a specific image in mind, or are you describing a mood?
- Do you have a deadline?

Once the scope of a project is established, a quote and an estimated completion time can be given.  Pricing is based on size, complexity, and materials.  Paintings in acrylic on canvas are the most common format, but other materials are possible.

### The Process

A deposit is required to begin work.  Brian shares progress photos as the piece develops.  You'll have an opportunity to weigh in before the painting is finished.

Completed pieces can be picked up locally (Fernandina Beach, FL) or shipped.  Shipping costs are in addition to the commission price.

### What to Know

- Brian doesn't make exact replicas of existing work. If you love a painting that's already sold, you can talk about something *in the spirit* of it.
- The process involves creative judgment. If you want total control over every element, a commission may not be the right fit.
- Rush work may be possible but carries an additional fee.
