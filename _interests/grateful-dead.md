The Grateful Dead are one of those phenomena that go well beyond music — there's an entire visual and cultural world attached to the band, and that world has been part of the fabric of Brian's artistic life for a long time.

From the skeletal iconography to the paisley explosions of the psychedelic poster tradition, the Dead produced (and inspired) some of the most imaginative visual art tied to any musical act. The *further* bus, the lightning-bolt skull, the dancing bears — these aren't just band logos, they're a mythology.
