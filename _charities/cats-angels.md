Cat's Angels is a Nassau County organization dedicated to rescuing, fostering, and rehoming cats. They do tireless work on a volunteer basis — trapping feral colonies, providing veterinary care, and finding permanent homes for animals that would otherwise have none.

Brian has supported their work through donated art and event participation. It's a cause that connects directly to the community and to the creatures that share it with us.
