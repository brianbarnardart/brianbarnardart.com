The North Atlantic right whale is one of the most endangered large mammals on Earth, with fewer than 400 individuals remaining. Right Whale Ambassadors works to raise awareness, support research, and advocate for the policy changes needed to prevent their extinction.

Living and working in Fernandina Beach — a stone's throw from the Atlantic — makes this cause feel close. These animals move through the waters off the Florida coast on their annual migration, and they need advocates.
